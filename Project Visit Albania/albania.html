About

Welcome to Visit Albania

Events

Best Festivals In Albania To Enjoy

Tirana International Film Festival
Tirana International Film Festival takes place each year in November. This cultural event was created in 2003 and is currently the most important cinematic event in Albania. Every year the festival is full of movie clips, full-length titles from all over the world, and many local films also showcased.
South Outdoor Festival
It celebrates sports, culture, tradition, music, nature and culinary. It gathers most popular outdoor activities in a event and it takes place every year in Borsh. It invites participants from all over the country and the whole Europe to join, compete and have fun, spread the joy and experience unique beauty of nature.
Korca Beer Festival
The Korca Beer Festival is a must-visit for four days every August if you are a lover of local and international beers. This is a hugely popular festival, with more than 100K people in attendance every year. It is free, and you wll get to sample beers from all over the world and some locally produced in the Korca region.
Albanian Independence Day
Albania has most significant public holiday takes place on 28 November and marks the day of independence. You will find countless celebrations going on around the country, and because most people are off work, there is likely to be something happening no matter where you are
